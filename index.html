EXCEPTIONAL VETERINARY SERVICES IN Gurugram

Partnering with International Experts for Your Pet's Well-being

Grounded in the Tata Trusts' values of compassion, courage, commitment, collaboration, and professionalism, we are an independent, ethical veterinary referral centre in Gurugram, Haryana, exclusively dedicated to dogs and cats.

ABOUT PAWFECT Vet Clinic

Situated within a multidisciplinary hospital, we leverage cutting-edge facilities and collaborate with specialists from various disciplines to diagnose and treat complex cases, ensuring the best outcomes for your beloved pets.

OUR FACILITIES

Spanning over 1,00,000 sq ft, our G+4 storey facility has been meticulously designed in consultation with international veterinary hospital architects. This ensures adherence to global best practices in hospital design and planning—guaranteeing the highest level of veterinary care for all small and companion animals.

OUR SPECIALITIES

Our experienced veterinarians provide thorough consultations to address any concerns or health issues your pet may have. We offer personalised care and treatment plans tailored to your pet's specific needs. Our Emergency and Critical Care Departments operate 24x7x365.
